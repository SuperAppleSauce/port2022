Managed Meta Accounts: Managing Integrations

Context

Managed Meta Accounts allow businesses to have a consolidated account structure for managing their various assets (like Facebook Pages, Instagram Accounts, ad accounts, etc.). It aims to provide more control, security, and access

The Problem

Businesses needed a way to manage integrations used within their organization, beyond just people and assets. This gap in our existing solution posed a significant risk to our users. In response, I led the design of a integration management feature, allowing businesses to view, approve, and manage integrations used within their accounts.

North Star

I developed a series of concepts that demonstrated the value of tool management within managed accounts. These concepts included early sketches, wireframes, and prototypes that illustrated how businesses could easily view, approve, and manage the tools being used within their accounts. My goal was to create a clear and compelling narrative that would get buy-in from our stakeholders.
