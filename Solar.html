Residential Solar Ownership

The Opportunity

Solar ownership is exciting in many ways! However, anxiety over affordability & schism of environmental impact keeps homeowners from believing their investment is worth while.

Focusing In On Ownership

For simplicity, this case study will be focused on the ownership experience in the greater solar experience. This where most management, financial, & enviromental concerns exist for new owners.

Two Motivated User Archetypes

Basic user

These users find elements of energy production, such as Kilowatt Hours, to be to advance and not relevant for them to understand how it impacts the enironment.

Advanced user

These users don’t feel like they have the information justify their purchase. This user manually calculates their savings by comparing old energy bills to the energy created, used, or sold by their solar array.

Common Theme & Opportunity

Both user archetypes ask themselves “was this worth it” As they are not a weigh the impact of energy production against their finacial or their sustainablity goals.
