About

Hi! I design things, but most importantly, I’m a people-person, and that’s the biggest reason why I love what I do. Whether it’s a user, a stakeholder, or a fellow team member, I incorporate their needs, their perspectives, and their values in everything I do. I’m a strong believer in an iterative process that involves frequent stakeholder visibility, data-driven decision-making, frequent co-creation, and constant user feedback. ‍
‍
In my free time, you can find me cultivating my vast collection of indoor plants, and tinkering with vintage electronics.
