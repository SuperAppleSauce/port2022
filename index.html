Lectures

Intro to Design Systems

Presented to Northwestern CS majors

An overview of design systems, their role in maintaining consistency across digital products, and how engineers can collaborate with designers to build scalable UI components. Covers key principles, real-world examples, and best practices for implementation.

Advanced Prototyping

Presented to NYU design majors

A deep dive into high-fidelity prototyping techniques, using tools like Figma, Principle, and Framer to create interactive experiences. Discusses the importance of rapid iteration, user testing, and bridging the gap between design and development.

The Design & Product Management Venn Diagram

Presented to NYU design majors

Explores the intersection of design and product management, highlighting how designers can think strategically and contribute to business goals. Covers frameworks for collaboration, decision-making, and balancing user needs with business objectives.

Crafting Design Narratives

Presented to NYU design majors

Focuses on storytelling in design—how to craft compelling case studies, present design work effectively, and communicate user insights to stakeholders. Emphasizes structure, persuasion, and visual storytelling techniques.

About Me

I’m a product designer and strategist with a deep passion for creating scalable, intuitive experiences. With a background in designing for millions of users at Meta and Verizon, I specialize in crafting systems that drive engagement, revenue, and user trust. My work has shaped third-party tool management, e-commerce flows, and trust & safety solutions—all while balancing business impact with user needs.


Beyond design, I thrive at the intersection of strategy and execution, influencing product roadmaps and leading cross-functional teams to bring bold ideas to life. Whether it's refining onboarding experiences, optimizing complex information architectures, or building interactive prototypes that push technical boundaries, I believe in progress before perfection—a mindset shaped by my time at Meta and my own ventures.


When I’m not designing, you can find me traveling the world, and chasing waves in remote surf spots.
